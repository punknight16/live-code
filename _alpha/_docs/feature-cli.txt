Feature Description

Who are the customers for this feature
	bloggers, authors, and non-technical entrepreneurs

What value will this feature bring to those customers
	they have a way to display their content

What value will this feature bring to our business

What are our assumptions

  2. These people could benefit from html publishing
  3. These people have money to publish.

What metrics will we measure to gague the success of the feature
